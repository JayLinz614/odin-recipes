BBQ Western Burger

This will be the best burger you will ever have in your life. It comes with nothing but the most premium toppings!

Everything you will need for this will be listed below.

  • Brioche bun
  • Onion chips
  • Pulled Pork
  • Extra cheese
  • Angus Beef Patty
  • Cold slaw

Cooking Instructions

  1. Pan Fry burger on 375 degree farenhiet for 10 mins
  2. Add cold slaw to bottom bun and mayo to top bun
  3. Then add patty to bottom bun
  4. Next add cheese and let melt ontop of patty
  5. Last add pulled pork and Onion chips
